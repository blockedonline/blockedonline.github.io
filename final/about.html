About Us
What we do

The BlockedOnline platform is a crowdsourcing approach to monitor the internet censorship. We intend to provide accurate information on internet censorship in all countries. One can explore internet censorship situation via BlockedOnline website either by country or by URL. For example, we can check in what countries is www.twitter.com cencored or we can see what websites areblocked in China.

Blockedonline was started by MIT students under supervision of Professor Tim Berners-Lee. Under assumption that there is no way to understand an end-userâ€™s internet situation from outside of their internet service, this approach requires volunteers to participate in gathering data from different locations. The process is developed to be as seamless as possible.

The long-term goal of the platform is to empower and raise awareness about the worldwide internet censorship by providing accurate data.

Links

BlockedOnline is inspired by and in close relationship with Herdict project at Berkman Center at Harvard University.

By the Client or Add-on

  • the time it takes for the Target Site to load,
  • your IP address,
  • Target Site content, as viewed on your computer, and

The Target Sites are independent, third-party websites which may collect information from your computer, such as your IP address. Any information collected by the Target Sites is governed by their policies and terms. BlockedOnline is not responsible for the information practices of the Target Sites.

If you have an existing relationship with a Target Site, we may inadvertently receive personal session data as part of the request to that site. This data will only be used to determine internet censorship, and we will not otherwise attempt to extract or utilize it. Except as described above, the Client and Add-on do NOT track your personal activities on your computer or browser.
